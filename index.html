I am a Senior Research Scientist in the Alibaba QuarkAudio Team. I lead the development of the QuarkAudio series, exploring a unified framework to handle different audio processing and generation tasks (speech, music, and general audio events).

🚀 Unified & Prompt-Free Handles multiple tasks without explicit instruction.
⚙️ Decoder-only AR-LM Backbone Leverages LLM-style autoregressive generation for speech token prediction.
🔄 End-to-End Compatible Integrates WavLM/Hubert, H-Codec, and LM into one unified pipeline.
🌍 Multitask Support SE, SR, TSE, SS, EDIT, VC, LASS, TTA, and more — all in a single model.
