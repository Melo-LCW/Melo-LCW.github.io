👋 About Me

I am a Senior Research Scientist at Alibaba Group. My research focuses on building unified foundation models for audio processing and generation, spanning speech enhancement (SE), super-resolution (SR), speech separation (SS), target speaker extraction (TSE), voice conversion (VC), and text-to-audio synthesis (TTA).

🚀 Unified & Prompt-Free Handles multiple audio tasks without explicit task instructions or prompts.
⚙️ Decoder-only AR-LM Leverages LLM-style autoregressive generation for speech token prediction.
🔄 End-to-End Pipeline Integrates WavLM/HuBERT, H-Codec, and LM into one unified system.
🌍 Multitask Support SE, SR, TSE, SS, EDIT, VC, LASS, TTA, and more — all in a single model.
